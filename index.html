About Us

Corporate governance ensures companies are run with transparency, accountability, and integrity. But extracting relevant data from long annual reports is often slow and inefficient.

That’s why our team developed a smart, web-based system named CGenius, to automate corporate governance data extraction. Our goal is to help organizations save time, improve compliance, and make better decisions through clear, accurate governance insights.

Problems

Governance Challenges

Extracting governance data from annual reports is harder than it seems — here are the key challenges organizations face today.

01

Time-Consuming

Manually reviewing lengthy annual reports can take hours or even days. This slows down reporting, delays decision-making, and drains valuable resources across teams.

02

Human-Error

Manual data extraction is prone to mistakes. Errors in interpreting or transcribing governance metrics can lead to compliance issues and weaken stakeholder trust.

03

Different Format

Each company presents annual reports differently — making it difficult to extract consistent governance data. Variations in layout and structure add complexity to the process.
